Key Projects

Automated Piano Playing Robot Hand

Ongoing

Droid Robotics Club

Developing a robotic hand capable of playing the piano, utilizing a mechanical algorithm to simulate human-like movements. The project aims to explore the intersection of robotics and music.

Autonomous Delivery Drone

Completed May 2023

Droid Robotics Club

Designed and implemented an autonomous drone capable of delivering packages to specified locations. The project involved programming navigation algorithms and optimizing drone stability and efficiency.

Smart Irrigation System

Completed January 2023

Droid Robotics Club

Created a smart irrigation system that uses sensors to monitor soil moisture levels and automate watering schedules. The system aims to optimize water usage and support sustainable agriculture.

AI-Powered Sorting Robot

Completed October 2022

Droid Robotics Club

Developed a robot that uses artificial intelligence to sort and categorize objects. The project involved training machine learning models and integrating them with the robot's hardware and software.

Obstacle Avoidance Robot

Completed June 2022

Droid Robotics Club

Built a robot equipped with sensors and algorithms to navigate through an obstacle course autonomously. The project focused on developing and testing various obstacle avoidance techniques.
